Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 12, 2026

Welcome to Candid Social! This app is built by a hobbyist developer with a simple goal: to provide a space for authentic sharing with your real friends, without the noise of a global feed.

Because privacy and trust are at the core of a close-knit social circle, this Privacy Policy explains exactly what information is collected, how it is used, and who helps process it.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect the essential information needed to make Candid Social work.

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address, display name, and profile photo.
  • User-Generated Content: We collect and store the images and videos you take and share through the Candid Social app.
  • What We DO NOT Collect: We do not collect your GPS location data, we do not request access to your phone’s address book or contacts, and we currently do not use any hidden tracking or analytics software to monitor your behavior in the app. Users find friends strictly by searching for usernames.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used strictly to provide the Candid Social experience. We use your information to:

  • Create and secure your account.
  • Display your profile and posts to the friends you have accepted into your circle.
  • Allow other users to search for your username so you can connect.

3. Third-Party Service Providers

To run Candid Social, we rely on trusted third-party service providers. We do not sell your data to advertisers or data brokers. We only share your information with these third parties for the strict purpose of hosting, maintaining, and securing the app. These categories of providers include:

  • Authentication Services: To securely manage your account creation and login process.
  • Cloud Hosting Providers: To run our app's servers and store basic text data, such as usernames and display names.
  • Cloud Storage Providers: To securely store and deliver the images and videos you share with your circle.

4. Who Can See Your Information

  • Your Friends: Because there is no global feed, the photos and videos you post are only visible to the users you have accepted as friends within the app.
  • Law Enforcement: We respect your privacy, but we will comply with legally binding requests for data from law enforcement agencies if required by law.

5. Children's Privacy

Candid Social is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 13. If we discover that a user under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will immediately delete their account and data.

6. Data Deletion and Your Rights

You own your data. If you want to leave Candid Social, you can request the complete deletion of your account, profile data, and all uploaded photos/videos.

To request data deletion, you can reach out via our in-app Support Page or email us directly at the address below.

7. Changes to This Policy

As Candid Social grows and evolves, this policy may be updated. If significant changes are made to how your data is handled, we will notify you within the app.
